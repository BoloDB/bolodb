## 2026-06-15 - Copy-to-clipboard success states
**Learning:** Adding immediate visual feedback (like changing icon, text, and styles) and `aria-live="polite"` to copy-to-clipboard buttons significantly reduces friction by providing a clear success state and screen reader announcement.
**Action:** Always include a visual success state and `aria-live="polite"` attribute when implementing copy-to-clipboard functionality to ensure it is clear, acknowledgeable, and accessible to screen readers.
